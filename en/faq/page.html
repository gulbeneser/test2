Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Serakıncı Limited and in which areas does it operate?

Serakıncı Limited is a company specializing in animal health and dermocosmetics. It serves as the sole authorized distributor in Northern Cyprus for globally recognized brands and provides quality-focused services.

Where can I procure your products?

You can obtain original products guaranteed by Serakıncı through our contracted pharmacies or veterinary clinics. Additionally, thanks to our partners, you can benefit from fast and reliable delivery options.

Are you operating as a distributor rather than conducting retail sales?

Yes, Serakıncı does not engage in direct retail sales. As an official distributor, we offer our products through pharmacies, veterinary clinics, and associated business partners in Northern Cyprus.

Do you provide an original product warranty?

Certainly. Thanks to authorised distributor agreements, all of our brands’ products are genuine and sourced through official channels. This eliminates the risk of counterfeit or imitation products entirely.

How does Serakıncı approach sustainability and animal welfare?

A significant proportion of the brands we operate with are recognised for their policies that prioritise environmentally friendly production and animal welfare. As Serakıncı, we also support these values, closely monitoring the quality and sustainability principles of the products we sell.

Whom can I contact for further information or support?

You can address your inquiries or collaboration requests to our team via the 'Contact Us' page or through our email address, (e.g.) [email protected]. We are delighted to assist you with any matters you may require assistance with.
